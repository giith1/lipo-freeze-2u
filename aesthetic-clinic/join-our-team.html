Desired Qualifications and Equipment:

Be part of our team at Lipo Freeze 2 U Surrey, working as a self employed consultant and running your own business.

You will play a central role in managing Tax and National Insurance affairs correctly. Remember we can't take responsible for any wrong transfer.

With freelance consultants, well, I would still have to have professional customer service, just as I would in a salon.

Uniform Policy:

During treatments we require you to wear suitable clothing worn, such as a salon tunic. Lipo Freeze 2 U does not supply a uniform, however we will let you know ahead of when your training is scheduled if you need to wear specific clothes.

Insurance Coverage:

Full coverage (full insurance coverage for all treatments is required). A valid insurance certificate may be requested to be shown.

Equipment & Accessories:

If you would like to be considered to be a therapist working with Lipo Freeze 2 U Surrey you will need to provide your own equipment to be able to carry out the treatments.

You must supply your own non medical equipment. If you want to expand your range of treatments, our suppliers can offer you low rates for products.

The Right To Refuse Treatment:

At Lipo Freeze 2 U Surrey, we are committed to the highest professional and ethical standards. We like our consultants to relax while they’re working for us.

There are reasons why some of these treatments will not be provided by our consultants, and you should be aware of the following:

– Broken skin or lesions

– Improper behavior, with acts of solicitation or suggestive comments

– Any undisclosed medical conditions

– Pregnancy if the treatment is considered unsuitable for pregnancy

– Intoxication from alcohol

Work Frequency:

The volume of work will depend on several factors such as the season, the demand in your area and the number of consultants working with us in that area. We freelance consultants cannot promise standard office hours.

Travel and Parking Expenses:

Travel expenses (unless otherwise directed to travel a great distance from base) are not included. The client is liable for parking costs and you should be reimbursed for all such costs upon receipt of payment for services.
