Lipo Freeze offers cutting-edge cryolipolysis, also known as fat freezing, a non-invasive treatment designed to specifically target and eliminate unwanted fat cells.

This innovative procedure works by using cold temperatures to break down body fat effectively. A specialized device delivers extreme cold to the fat cells, causing them to freeze, die, and be naturally eliminated through the lymphatic system.

Cryolipolysis is ideal for sculpting troublesome areas such as the lower abdomen and upper arms. While not a weight loss solution, it delivers noticeable body contouring results.
